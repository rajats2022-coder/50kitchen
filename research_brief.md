## Site Direction

The new website should sell three things at once:

1. Chef Anthony's story: faith, second chances, Dorchester roots, Chopped/Martha Stewart, press credibility.
2. Food truck convenience: find the next stop, order online, follow Instagram.
3. Catering revenue: corporate lunches, private events, breweries, festivals, weddings, and group packages.

- Menu: recreated food truck menu with filters, prices, allergens.

## Launch Notes / Needs Owner Confirmation

- Confirm current primary business location: public sources conflict between Pittsboro, Chapel Hill, Durham, and location-varies food truck language. Draft uses safest wording: Triangle Area food truck.
- Confirm current menu/prices/allergens before launch.
- Confirm schedule entries before launch; ideally connect a CMS/calendar source.
- Replace mailto form with production form backend or CRM.
- Confirm permission to use current website images and press references.
- Add more high-quality food/truck/event photos from Instagram or owner-provided assets for final polish.
